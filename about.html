A Message from the Co-Secretary-Generals

Dear Delegates,

It is our honour to welcome you to YLSMUN 2026. As Co-Secretary-Generals, we are proud to present a conference that represents dedication, hard work, and a shared belief in the power of young leaders.

Our Purpose

YLSMUN 2026 has been designed with one clear goal—to offer all delegates a fair, professional, and meaningful Model UN experience. We believe that leadership is built through consistent learning, respectful dialogue, and the confidence to express one's ideas. Our conference aims to support you in developing these important skills.

What Makes YLSMUN 2026 Special

This year, we are proud to host North India's first fully youth-led and independent MUN. Every aspect of the conference—from academics to logistics—has been prepared by student leaders who are committed to creating a high-quality and unbiased environment.

At YLSMUN 2026, you can expect:

  • Well-prepared committees with relevant and engaging agendas
  • A fair and knowledgeable Executive Board
  • Smooth and organized logistics
  • Delegate kits designed to enhance your experience
  • A respectful and positive environment for everyone

Our Commitment to You

We maintain a strict zero-tolerance policy toward any form of bias or misconduct. If any issues arise, our team will address them immediately and transparently. Your comfort, safety, and learning experience remain our top priorities.

Looking Ahead

We encourage every delegate to participate actively, think creatively, and communicate confidently. Whether this is your first MUN or your twentieth, we hope YLSMUN 2026 becomes an experience that teaches you, inspires you, and stays with you.

We look forward to welcoming you soon.

Warm regards,
Yuval & Yajas
Co-Secretary-Generals, YLSMUN 2026
